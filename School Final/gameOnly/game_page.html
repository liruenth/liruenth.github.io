Let's Play a Dragon Game!!

A terrible thing has happened. While a mother dragon was off hunting for food in anticipation of her eggs hatching the eggs have rolled into a canyon that she can't get in.
Worse still dragon eggs smell very strongly and are highly desired by men and other beasts.
Protect the eggs by shooting fire at the invaders. Over time the eggs will hatch and the hatchlings will help you fight.
click here to go back.

Controls:

  • Aim with the mouse and press "m" to breathe a fire ball.
  • Each dragon can shoot 1 fire ball at a time. The more hatchlings you save the more fire balls you can shoot at a time.
  • Enemies appear from the top and work their way down to the eggs. For each that succeeds you will lose an egg.
  • Some enemies are harder to defeat than others. The number of shots it takes to defeat one is posted above them.
  • Aim well and may you ever be a friend of the dragons.
